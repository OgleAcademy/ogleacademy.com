Welcome to Ogle Academy

Ogle Academy is a dedicated homeschool committed to delivering a comprehensive K-12 education in compliance with Texas Education Agency requirements. We provide personalized learning experiences to foster academic excellence and good citizenship.

About Us

Based in Texas, Ogle Academy provides high-quality homeschool education. Our program adheres to Texas homeschooling laws, ensuring a bona fide curriculum that meets state standards for K-12 education.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum includes the required subjects of reading, spelling, grammar, mathematics, and good citizenship, as outlined by the Texas Education Agency. We tailor our approach to meet the individual needs of each student, preparing them for future success.
